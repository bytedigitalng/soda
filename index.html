The Spirit of Daniel Academy

Where learning is fun and excellence is normal

The SODA vision is to be a unique centre of academic excellence with a reputation for the production of leaders for Nigeria, Africa and indeed the rest of the world.

The Spirit of Daniel Academy (SODA) is a modern technology-enabled secondary school with a strong Christian ethos and a commitment to the development of young men and women with excellent character, mental fortitude and a solid sense of responsibility.

OUR AIM

Making students love learning through the adoption of exciting learning technologies and teaching concept

The SODA vision is to be unique centre of academic excellence with a reputation for the production of leaders for Nigeria, Africa and indeed the rest of the world

To develop a world class integrated and consistent school workforce, working with a crop of skilled, professional, knowledgeable teachers and non-academic workers.

To adopt easy to use, affordable and efficient technology as learning tools to accelerate learning and self-development skills

THE METHOD

It is the age of the digital global village; you can learn anything from anywhere. Each class is equipped with technology to enhance learning.

  • At SODA, teachers bring lessons to life using modern technology.
  • Lessons are cutting edge and are adapted to reflect modern day to day global activities and developments.

Away with teachers who dictate notes for children to copy.

Away with students spending time to copy notes instead of learning.

We cannot solve 21st century problems using 19th century solutions. We are in the 21st century.
