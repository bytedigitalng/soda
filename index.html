The Spirit of Daniel Academy

Where learning is fun and excellence is normal

The SODA vision is to be a unique centre of academic excellence with a reputation for the production of leaders for Nigeria, Africa and indeed the rest of the world.

The Spirit of Daniel Academy (SODA) is a modern technology-enabled secondary school with a strong Christian ethos and a commitment to the development of young men and women with excellent character, mental fortitude and a solid sense of responsibility.
